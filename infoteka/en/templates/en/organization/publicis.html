Publicis Groupe

Publicis was founded in France by Marcel Bleustein-Blanchet in 1926. He was an extraordinary man who also fought in the Second World War and pioneered radio advertising in France.

The company’s origins were quite modest – it all started in a Paris apartment. Today, after over 90 years of development, the company is now the group – Publicis Groupe – the 3rd largest communication group in the world.


Holding of the year 2017

For the first time in the 17-year history of the magazine "Media and Marketing Polska", Publicis was named "Holding of the Year". The editors clearly rated Publicis Groupe very highly. This special title was testament to our great business results and positive transformations in terms of cooperation between agencies, as well as the quality of our services and achievements in the digital field. That was indeed quite something!


Power of ONE

The philosophy of the group is THE POWER OF ONE. All units should act in synergy and complement their competences to build real value for the client by providing the best business and communication solutions.

The main areas of activity are: communication strategy, film production, media planning and buying, e-commerce, performance marketing, content marketing, public relations, public affairs, CSR, and consulting services in the field of business transformation.



Publicis Communication

Publicis Communications is a centre of creative expertise and Publicis’ real right hand in advertising.The main areas of our activity are creation, film production, public relations and public affairs. Our job is to attract the greatest talents in the industry and provide clients with effective solutions in the field of communication in marketing.

We create tailor-made answers based on consumer insights, brand knowledge and creative concepts, with added data and technology support. PC employs 30,000 experts in 100 branches across 20 countries.

In Poland we operate the agencies: Saatchi & Saatchi IS, Leo Burnett, Publicis Worldwide, MSL, Prodigious, Marcel and Fallon.


Publicis Media

In terms of turnover Publicis Media represents the leading large group of agencies in Poland (RECMA ranking). It has also received the title Most Desired Employer (Antal company ranking).

Its main areas of activity are: communication strategy, media planning and buying, performance marketing, analytics and research, technology and data management, content marketing and business transformation. Thanks to the continuous cooperation of its different agencies, which make use of their varied resources and expert knowledge, the group guarantees easy access to all of its key competences and services to meet clients' business needs. PM employs 18,000 experts in 285 branches across 80 markets.

In Poland the following agencies operate within its framework: Starcom, Zenith, Digitas, Spark, Blue 449, Performics and LiquidThread.

Publicis Sapient

Companies must be modern and competitive to meet today's market requirements, and this involves the adoption of a completely new thinking about business – digital transformation. Publicis Sapient, building a business platform from scratch, changes both a client's business strategy and his operational activity, making possible improvements in performance and his readiness for further development and innovation.
